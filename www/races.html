Races

62 playable races, measured from the live game code (2026-07-17). Race decides far more than looks: attribute dice, whether you are a Mega-Damage creature or flesh and blood, innate magic and psionics, and how the world treats you. A Great Horned Dragon hatchling starts with hundreds of MDC and can metamorph into human form to fit human-sized armor; a human starts with 3d6 attributes and a much longer list of career options. Races marked as RCCs (Racial Character Classes) are a class in themselves and forgo an OCC.
