Play in browser

Browser client for AetherMUD. It talks WebSocket to a local bridge on port 1129, which relays to the MUD telnet port 1122. Prefer Mudlet or telnet for the best experience; this client is a convenient fallback.

Disconnected

If connect fails: the MUD must be running (./mud.sh start) and the WebSocket bridge must be listening on 1129 (started automatically by mud.sh). Firewall: allow TCP 1122 and 1129. After login, type colorize for ANSI color.
