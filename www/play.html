How to connect

AetherMUD is played with a telnet or MUD client, not a browser. Point your client at the host below on port 1122.

Connect

ClientHostPort
Mudletlocalhost1122 (telnet)
telnetlocalhost1122
telnet localhost 1122

Recommended: Mudlet is a free, cross-platform MUD client with good ANSI color support and scripting. Any telnet client also works. After login, type colorize for ANSI color.
