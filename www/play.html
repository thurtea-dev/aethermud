How to connect

Connect with a telnet or MUD client on port 1122.

Connect

ClientHostPort
Mudletaethermud.com1122 (telnet)
telnetaethermud.com1122
telnet aethermud.com 1122

Recommended: Mudlet is a free, cross-platform MUD client with good ANSI color support and scripting. Any telnet client also works. After login, type colorize for ANSI color.

About the game

The Great Cataclysm shattered Earth's golden age, and the ley lines erupted with the magic of a dying world. Rifts opened to a thousand alien dimensions, and everything came through. Play a Coalition grunt, a Ley Line Walker, a Juicer burning bright, or a dragon hatchling wearing a borrowed human shape. Explore Chi-Town, Tolkeen, the Splynn Dimensional Market, and New Camelot; ride Moxim's rifts between continents. Start with the world guide, races, and Classes.

Measured from the live mudlib (2026-07-23):

  • 62 playable races, 66 Classes
  • 115 spells, 51 psionic powers, 157 skills
  • ~430 playable Rifts rooms across Praxis, Chi-Town, Tolkeen, Horton, New Camelot, and NGR Germany, plus stub zones (world guide with honest zone status)
  • 174 mortal commands; custom prompt with MDC/SDC/PPE/ISP pool tokens

In-game help is two-level: help lists categories; help <category> lists topics; help <topic> opens a topic (separator-insensitive: armor of ithan matches armor_of_ithan). Use help index for the flat list.
