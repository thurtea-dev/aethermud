Commands

Every mortal command in AetherMUD, one line each, grouped by what it's for. Type commands in game for a plain column list, or help <topic> for detail on any one of these.

Movement and world interaction

CommandWhat it does
lookLook at the room, an object, or a direction. Handles darkness.
examineLong description of an object, plus condition and value.
get / takePick up an item.
dropDrop an item or money.
giveHand an item or money to another player or NPC.
pushPush an object. Some pushes trigger a hidden result.
digDig at certain Splynn locations with a wielded shovel.
faceTurn to face a direction or a person.
left / rightSet your active hand, for dual-wielding and directional actions.
whereSite-based location lookup.
sitesManage or view your allowed login sites.

Communication

CommandWhat it does
saySpeak aloud. Routes through the language system.
shoutShout to the whole domain. Five second cooldown, no language filter.
whisperSend a private message to someone in the same room.
tellSend a private message to any player, local or intermud (user@mud).
replyReply to your last tell.
emote / pemoteFree-form emote, or a possessive one ("Bob's sword glows").
converseConversation mode: repeated say without retyping "say".
speakSpeak in a specific language you know.
language / languagesSwitch your active spoken language, or list the ones you know.
tonguesCasts Tongues if you know it, otherwise reports what you understand.
oocGlobal out-of-character channel. ooc off to stop receiving.
radioTransmit or receive on a carried radio.
watchradioToggle whether you receive radio transmissions.
cardSet or show a text business card, to the room or to one player.
greet / introduceGreet someone, or formally introduce yourself so you both learn each other's name.
remember / forgetAdd or remove a player from your face list.
knownList players you currently recognize.
request title <title>Ask an RP-Wizard for an RP prefix title.
echoRoleplay room echo, logged for staff review.
harassToggle session logging of abusive behavior, for later staff review.
snoopableGrant or revoke permission for low-level staff to snoop you.
fingerShow a player's finger info. Supports remote user@mud lookups.
chfnChange your own finger info.
peerPeer around the room or in a direction. Light-level gated.

Combat

CommandWhat it does
killStart melee combat against a target.
assistJoin combat on an ally's side.
shoot / fireStart ranged combat with a wielded ranged weapon.
fleeAttempt to flee. Costs 1 APM, PP-based chance, minimum 30%.
stopEnd your attacks.
dodge / parryToggle shortcuts for autododge and autoparry.
autododgeToggle spending 1 APM per attack to dodge instead of free autoparry.
autoparryToggle automatic free parry on incoming attacks. On by default.
stanceSet your combat stance: offensive, defensive, or standard.
combatDisplay your current combat statistics.
assassinate / assassinationProwl/sneak ambush strike with a blade. Requires an assassin-type OCC.
reloadLoad an e-clip into a ranged weapon.
unloadRemove remaining ammo from a weapon into a partial e-clip.
wimpySet your auto-flee HP percent threshold.
wimpydirSet the direction you flee toward when wimpy triggers.
sneak / hideRoll Prowl to move silently.
treat / bandageRoll First Aid to heal a target 1d6 HP or SDC.
bodyShow your damage pools (MDC, or HP/SDC) and any narratively missing limbs.
saving_throwsShow your saving throw values.

Magic and psionics

CommandWhat it does
cast <spell>Cast a known spell, optionally at <target>.
psi <power>Use a known psionic power, optionally at <target>.
spells / psionicsList your known spells, or your known psionic powers.
psihelpJump into the help system for psionics topics.
telepathy, empathy, mindblock, objectread, senseevil, telekinesisDirect shortcuts for those specific psionic powers.
psiswordCyber-Knight only: manifest a psychic blade for 10 ISP.
remoteviewPsychically locate a known player by name.
readauraRead a living target's true race and level in the same room; pierces disguises and appearance-override armor. Costs ISP.
armorofithanShortcut to cast Armor of Ithan.
magicnetShortcut to cast or trigger Magic Net on your current opponent.

Character info and progression

CommandWhat it does
scoreYour compact character sheet.
statsYour physical attribute scores.
abilitiesYour racial and OCC special abilities.
experienceYour XP and progress to next level.
advanceAdvance a level: rolls HP/MDC gain, grants PPE/ISP per your OCC table.
improveSpend a pending skill slot to raise a known skill.
skillsList all your known skills, grouped by category.
skillrequestNumbered skill picker for your OCC; request or cancel a pending grant.
roll <skill>Roll d100 against a named skill.
bionicsDisplay your installed cybernetics.
eq / equipment / wornShow worn gear and wielded weapons by slot.
inventoryList what you're carrying.
money / creditsShow currency on hand.
exchangeSmuggler-OCC credit and black-market exchange.
faction / reputationShow your standing with major Rifts factions.
wantedCheck your own infamy score and wanted status.
kills / lastkillerYour kill count, or who last killed you.
biographyFull write-up: birth date, marriage, kills, quests.
questsTrack active and completed quests.
customizeMenu-driven appearance editor.
describeSet or clear your player description.
pictureStore or display a small ASCII portrait.
position <text>Set an RP position string shown in room descriptions.
clanJoin or inquire about the Aerihman clan.
metamorphShapeshift, for GHD, changeling, and vampire races.
breathUse a racial breath weapon on a target.
flyToggle racial flight.
telemechanicsRacial interface-with-machines ability.
tattoo / touchList, activate, or check status of Atlantean tattoo magic.

Roleplay, social, and economy

CommandWhat it does
guildCreate, invite, accept, decline, kick, leave, bank, or toggle your guild tag.
tradeStructured player-to-player trade session.
slaveSplynn only: list or free slaves you own.
mailList, read, send, delete, or clear player mail.
forwardToggle forwarding your mail to another player.
riftGlobal rift travel to a purchased destination.
driveDrive a Tier-1 vehicle. Needs fuel and a vehicle-accessible room.
pilotMount, check status of, or dismount a vehicle. Needs the matching pilot skill.
ejectEject from a vehicle, or quickly remove power armor.
store / retrieveStore or retrieve items in an active dimensional pocket.
rest / wakeRest to heal at Rifts tabletop rates, in rooms that allow it. Wake to stand up.
meditateRestore PPE on a ley line, or ISP anywhere. Ten minute cooldown.
setrespawnSet your respawn point to the current room. Room must allow it.

System and utility

CommandWhat it does
helpCategory and topic help menu.
commandsList available commands in columns.
faqPage through the FAQ.
newsReplay global login news.
dateShow the current mud time.
versionShow mud, driver, and mudlib version, plus uptime.
nextrebootShow time until the next scheduled reboot.
clsClear an ANSI terminal screen.
color / colorizeToggle ANSI color on or off.
briefToggle short room descriptions.
promptView or set a custom command prompt.
sbarToggle a post-move HP/SDC/MDC/PPE/ISP status bar.
password / passwdInteractively change your password.
idleShow how long a player has been idle.
whoPlayer list. Mortals see staff tags and a population count.
usersSimple list of connected, non-invisible player names.
mudlist / mudparty / rwhoKnown intermud muds; join or start an intermud party; remote rwho query.
suicidePermanently delete a level 3 or lower character. Requires typed confirmation.
bug / idea / typo / mudidea / praiseFile a bug, idea, typo, cross-mud idea, or compliment report to staff.
