Character creation

Room: /domains/Praxis/setter.c. Guide NPC: chargen_guide.

Steps

  1. Zone: start americas, start atlantis, or start europe
  2. Attributes: pick roll, optional pick reroll (up to 4 rerolls)
  3. Race: read list, pick <race>
  4. Alignment: alignment 1 through alignment 7
  5. OCC: list, pick occ <name>, or no occ
  6. Elective skills: skills <category>, pick skill <name>
  7. Secondary skills (if granted)
  8. Finish: starting package, languages, teleport to welcome room

Example

> start americas
> pick roll
> pick human
> alignment 2
> pick occ headhunter
> skills weapons
> pick skill tracking

Start rooms

  • Americas: /domains/Praxis/rifts_welcome
  • Atlantis: /domains/Praxis/areas/splynn/splynn_entry
  • Europe: /domains/newcamelot/areas/camelot_gate
