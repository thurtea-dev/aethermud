Occupational Character Classes

66 OCCs, measured from the live game code (2026-07-17). Your OCC is your trade: it sets your attacks per melee, skill selections, starting equipment, and PPE or ISP pools. Some have attribute requirements; a Cyber-Knight demands more of a body than a Vagabond does. Racial Character Classes (dragons, vampires, Mind Melters, and kin) take no OCC at all; see Races. The count includes a few alias names in the daemon list (for example mercenary / coalition soldier); the careers below are the distinct classes.
